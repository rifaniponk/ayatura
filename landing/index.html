Questions

Quietly answered.

Do I have to memorize the whole Quran to use Ayatura?+
Not at all. Ayatura works with whatever you've memorized, five surahs or five hundred. The rotation balances across exactly what you've added.
Does it work without internet?+
Yes, your surah pool, monthly plan, prayer times and widget are all computed and stored on your device. The only feature that needs internet is the in-app Quran reader, which pulls text and translations from quran.com.
What if a surah is too long for one prayer?+
You can add a specific ayat range for any surah (e.g. Al-Baqarah 1–6). The plan treats that range as one rotating unit.
Can I lock or change a day's plan?+
Yes. Any day or prayer can be locked so a regenerate won't touch it. You can also shuffle a single prayer.
Where does the Quran text come from?+
Ayah text and translations are fetched live from quran.com when you open the in-app reader. We don't modify the text. You're reading the same Uthmani source millions of Muslims use every day.
How much does Ayatura cost?+
Nothing. Ayatura is free, with no subscriptions and no in-app purchases. It's offered as sadaqah jariyah.
