Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026

Overview

Ayatura ("the app") is a daily Surah planner for Muslims memorizing the Quran. It is developed and maintained by ponkcoding.

We built Ayatura to respect your privacy. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Everything you add, including your Surah selections, hifdh progress, and prayer schedule, stays on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

Ayatura does not collect, access, or share any of the following:

Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number), location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, crash reports, or any other information that could be used to identify you.

The app does not require an account and does not connect to the internet.

Data Stored on Your Device

All data you enter into Ayatura (Surah plans, prayer slots, hifdh entries) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is never sent to us or any third party.

Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data.

Waitlist (Website Only)

The Ayatura website (ayatura.app) offers an optional email waitlist so we can notify you when the app launches. Submitting your email is entirely voluntary. If you sign up, your email address is stored securely via Netlify Forms and will only be used to send you launch updates. We will not share it with third parties or use it for marketing beyond those updates.

To request removal from the waitlist, email us at ponkcoding@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Ayatura does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it poses no privacy risk to children.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever add features that change how data is handled, we will update this page and revise the effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at ponkcoding@gmail.com.
